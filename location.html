Location

Hotels

Some hotels near the venue are listed below. Reservations are possible by phone or by mail. Please always indicate participation in the conference "Open Up DSE" when making a reservation. Participants will receive a discounted rate.

  • Plattenhof Hotel

Would you like to stay at another place? The conference takes place in the centre of Zurich, not far from the railway station, where many other accommodations are available.

Transportation

Getting to the Conference Venue from Zurich Main Station (Zürich HB):

  1. Take Tram No. 10 (towards the airport) from Zurich Bahnhofplatz/HB.
  2. OR Take Tram No. 6 (towards the zoo) from Bahnhofstrasse/HB.

Coming from Zurich Airport:

  1. Take the train via the main station.
  2. OR Take Tram No. 10 directly to ETH/University Hospital.

From ETH/University Hospital Station to the Conference Venue:

  1. Alight at ETH/University Hospital station.
  2. Proceed past the main building of ETH.
  3. After approximately 250 meters, find the main building of UZH on the right side.
  4. The conference office is on the first floor in room KOL-F-122.
  5. Pay attention to on-site signage for guidance.
